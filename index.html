Temiloluwa Adeniyi

I'm a biomedical engineer and data scientist, as well as a recent graduate of Georgia Tech. I have done work in these areas for the past several years in the capacity of an academic researcher, engineering consulting manager, freelancer, and also through my startup, Nopneu.

Within data science, I specialize in statistical analysis, cleaning, visualizations, machine learning models, public policy and also developing data governance strategies. My preferred languages are Python and R, along with a wide range of packages that come with both. However, I'm comfortable with using other data science languages as well, such as SPSS and Matlab that I have used in other projects. Please check out my porfolio with some past projects for example work, as well as a more comprehensive list of the languages and packages I've worked with on projects.

Within medical devices, I specialize in regulatory and quality control, but I am able to work in any other areas of the product life cycle, as well. In past projects, I've worked on class 1, class 2 and class 3 devices for several areas of medical devices. These areas included software applications, dermatological, diagnostic biotech, diagnostic radiology, orthopedic, cardiac, urology, and physical therapy devices. Some of the regulatory documents completed have included: design history folders, risk assessments, 510 (k), DIOVV, quality plans, and verification/validation testing. Please feel free to contact me for further discussion.
